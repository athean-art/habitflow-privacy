The short version

NoiseBlend Pro collects zero personal data. All sounds play locally on your device. No account required. No internet needed. No ads. No tracking.

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Introduction

NoiseBlend Pro ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices for the NoiseBlend Pro Android application published on Google Play.

2. Data We Collect

We collect no personal data whatsoever. Specifically, NoiseBlend Pro does NOT:

  • Collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Collect usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Use advertising networks or tracking SDKs
  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Require an account or registration of any kind
  • Access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location
  • Stream audio from the internet (all sounds are bundled in the app)

3. Data Stored on Your Device

Your preferences (selected sounds, volume levels, timer settings) are stored locally on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device to our servers (there are no servers)
  • May be included in Android's own device backup if you have Google Backup enabled
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

4. Permissions Explained

🔔
POST_NOTIFICATIONS To display sleep timer notifications showing when audio will stop. Required on Android 13+.
🔊
FOREGROUND_SERVICE / WAKE_LOCK To keep audio playing while your screen is off. No data is collected through this permission.

5. Third-Party Services

NoiseBlend Pro does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app has no SDKs that track you.

6. Audio Content

All audio content (white noise, nature sounds, ambient sounds) is bundled within the app itself. No audio is streamed from the internet. No microphone access is required or requested.

7. Children's Privacy

NoiseBlend Pro is suitable for all ages including children. Since we collect no data at all, there are no special concerns for users under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
