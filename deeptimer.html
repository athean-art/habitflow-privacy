The short version

DeepTimer collects zero personal data. Your focus sessions stay on your device. No account required. No internet connection needed. No ads. No tracking. Ever.

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. Introduction

DeepTimer ("the App", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices for the DeepTimer Android application published on Google Play.

2. Data We Collect

We collect no personal data whatsoever. Specifically, DeepTimer does NOT:

  • Collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Collect usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Use advertising networks or tracking SDKs
  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Require an account or registration of any kind
  • Access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location
  • Connect to the internet at all (the app has no network permission)

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Session history (timestamps, duration, session type — Pomodoro or Deep Work)
  • App settings (timer durations, sound and vibration preferences, theme)

This data:

  • Never leaves your device to our servers (there are no servers)
  • May be included in Android's own device backup if you have Google Backup enabled (this is entirely controlled by your Android settings)
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

4. Permissions Explained

🔔
POST_NOTIFICATIONS To display timer progress and phase-complete alerts (e.g., "Break time!"). Required on Android 13+.
⚙️
FOREGROUND_SERVICE To keep the timer running accurately while the app is in the background or screen is off. No data is collected through this permission.
⏰
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM To deliver timer notifications at the exact moment a phase ends. All scheduling is local — nothing is sent anywhere.
📱
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED To restore any scheduled notifications after your device restarts.
📳
VIBRATE To vibrate the device when a Pomodoro phase changes (optional — can be disabled in Settings).

These permissions are used solely for local timer functionality. No data is transmitted anywhere.

5. Third-Party Services

DeepTimer does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services. The app contains no trackers of any kind. It does not use Firebase, Google Analytics, Crashlytics, Mixpanel, or any similar service.

6. Children's Privacy

DeepTimer is suitable for all ages. Since we collect no data at all, there are no special concerns for users under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR).

7. Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply uninstall the app from your device. All locally stored session history and settings will be permanently and immediately removed.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
