Privacy Policy

The short version

HabitFlow collects zero personal data. Everything stays on your device. No account required. No internet connection needed. No ads. No tracking.

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Introduction

HabitFlow ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices for the HabitFlow Android application published on Google Play.

2. Data We Collect

We collect no personal data whatsoever. Specifically, HabitFlow does NOT:

  • Collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Collect usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Use advertising networks or tracking SDKs
  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Require an account or registration of any kind
  • Access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All app data — your habits, completion records, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Android's built-in storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device to our servers (there are no servers)
  • May be included in Android's own device backup if you have Google Backup enabled (this is entirely controlled by your Android settings)
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

4. Permissions Explained

🔔
POST_NOTIFICATIONS To display your habit reminder notifications. Required on Android 13+.
⏰
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM To deliver reminders at the exact time you set. All scheduling is local.
📱
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED To restore scheduled reminders after your device restarts.
📳
VIBRATE To vibrate the device when a notification is delivered.

These permissions are used solely for local functionality. No data is transmitted anywhere.

5. Third-Party Services

HabitFlow does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app has no SDKs that track you.

The optional "Share Streak" feature uses Android's native share sheet. Any content you share is at your discretion and subject to the privacy policies of the app you share to.

6. Children's Privacy

HabitFlow is suitable for all ages. Since we collect no data at all, there are no special concerns for users under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
